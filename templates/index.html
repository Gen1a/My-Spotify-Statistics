Features

Profile Overview

Get a nice overview of your Spotify Profile, eg. your followers, followed artists, saved playlists, saved tracks, recently played tracks...

Spotify Footprint

Always wanted to know what your Top Tracks, Top Artists and Top Genres are? This feature will give you access to all 3, ranging from short term (4 weeks) to long term (all time)!

Analytics

This will give you access to both Track Analytics (for a single track) and Playlist Analytics (for one of your saved playlists). Get deeper insight in a track or playlist's combined audio features like danceability, instrumentalness, liveness, tempo and loudness.

Party Planner

Create a unique playlist which will suit every party guest by combining your guests' top artists' top tracks, all this in just a few clicks.

Running Tool

Struggling to find a playlist which perfectly suits your running tempo? This tool will create a playlist which consists of songs of roughly the same beats per minute (bpm) in your preferred genre (COMING SOON!)
